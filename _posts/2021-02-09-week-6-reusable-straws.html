There are plenty of alternatives to the single-use plastic straw. Here are the pros and cons of five different materials. Of course, if you have any bamboo handy, you could always make your own!

BAMBOO

  1. PROS

    • 100% Biodegradable
    • Don't conduct heat or cold
    • Inexpensive

    CONS

    • Hardest to clean
    • Shortest lifespan
    • Sometimes has woody aftertaste

GLASS

  1. PROS

    • Easiest to Clean/Dishwasher Safe
    • No Added Taste
    • Hypoallergenic
    • Can be added to curbside glass recycling

    CONS

    • Most Breakable
    • Most Expensive
    • Conductor of Hot and Cold Liquids

STAINLESS STEEL

  1. PROS

    • Most Durable
    • Can Be Recycled with Metals
    • Travel Friendly

    CONS

    • Temperature Sensitive
    • Difficult to clean without straw cleaner
    • More expensive than other options

SILICONE

  1. PROS

    • Flexible
    • Inexpensive
    • Once burned, silicone ash is biodegadable

    CONS

    • Difficult to clean
    • Not biodegradable

PLASTIC

  1. PROS

    • Inexpensive

    CONS

    • Plastic never breaks down
    • Plastic toxins are harmful
    • Usually not recyclable
