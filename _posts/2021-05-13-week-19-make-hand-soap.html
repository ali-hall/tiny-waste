Castile soap is non-toxic, organic, vegetable based jack of all trades. It can be used to clean your body, your house, and even your pets! Castile soap comes unscented or in a variety of all natural scents and you can purchase a half gallon jug from Package Free Shop for $60. Yes, this bulk castile soap comes in a recyclable plastic bottle, but it is still a natural, low-waste alternative to many little hand-soap bottles.

What you'll need:

  • Liquid Castile Soap - I like Dr
  • Water
  • (Optional) Essential Oil. If you are using scented castile soap, this is especially unnecessary.
  • Small bottle with pump. I used an empty bottle that I already had.
  • Funnel for pouring
