There are loads of reusable water bottle options out there

PLASTIC

  1. PROS

    CONS

    • Lightweight
    • Inexpensive
    • No temperature control
    • Easily Accumulates Mold
    • Short life span
    • Plastic can leach into water

GLASS

  1. PROS

    CONS

    • Easy to clean
    • Glass is infinitely recyclable
    • Well insulated
    • Long-lasting
    • More expensive
    • Heavy
    • Not allowed everywhere

ALUMINUM

  1. PROS

    CONS

    • Affordable
    • Lightweight
    • Long-lasting
    • Dents easily

STAINLESS STEEL

  1. PROS

    CONS

    • Doesn't leach chemicals
    • Well insulated
    • Durable
    • Rust and mold resistant
    • Can be heavy
    • More expensive
    • Sometimes has metallic aftertaste

  1. PROS

    CONS

    • Collapsible
    • Inexpensive
    • Breaks down
