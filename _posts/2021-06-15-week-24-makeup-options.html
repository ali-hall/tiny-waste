This week I took a good look at my beauty routine and its environmental impact. After taking a look at my makeup drawer, I realized just how much plastic waste I've produced over the last twenty years. As I use up the last of my conventional makeup, it's time to find a low-waste, eco-friendly replacement. Here are ten sustainable brands I've found that cut back on plastic packaging and use natural ingredients.

What they offer: 3-in-1 Crayons that can be used on lips, cheeks, and eyes.

Price: One balmie starts at $14, 3 balmies for $36 ($6 savings), or 14 for $96 ($100 savings).

Axiology has been featured in Elle, Vogue, Oprah, and InStyle for a good reason. These balmies aren't your average product; they come in 14 colors and can be used as hydrating lip balm, blush, and eye shadow. The best part is that they come in thin compostable paper wrap, making them the lowest waste product on this list. These balmies are also vegan, cruelty-free, and made using all-natural ingredients.

River Organics

{% include image-source-set.html image="24-makeup/river-organics-makeup.jpg" alt="river organics brand lip balm, blush and highlighter in paperboard tubes" %}

What they offer: Everything

Price: $10-$18 for single products

River Organics is a rare find in the cosmetics world in that practically all of their products come in biodegradable paper packaging. They even offer a plastic-free mascara cake that can be applied with a reusable wand. River Organics products are non-toxic, vegan, and cruelty-free.

Elate Cosmetics

{% include image-source-set.html image="24-makeup/elate-cosmetics-makeup.jpg" alt="elate cosmetics brand eye shadow in bamboo case and bamboo brow brush" %}

What they offer: Everything

Price: Refill inserts start at $15

Elate Cosmetics carries everything you need for a sustainable makeup overhaul. They've done away with the majority of plastic and instead offer refillable bamboo compacts as well as recyclable aluminum and glass containers. They even has vegan makeup brushes with bamboo and aluminum handles.

As a great bonus, they offer sample kits so you can find your perfect concealer and foundation. The samples come in recyclable/reusable aluminum tins that are perfect for making your own lip balm.
