I bought these trash cans at Lowes for $5.00/each. You can use ANY kind of container for this...cardboard boxes, storage bins

  1. upload images

  2. size image

  3. Cut the thing on cricut

  4. Remove the background

  5. use a pick or tweezers for the smaller sections

  6. apply transfer tape

  7. pull it away

  8. line up where you want

  9. remove transfer tape

  10. you did it
