Are wooden chopsticks ok?

While wooden chopsticks are biodegradable, they aren't as innocent as they seem. Globally, 80 billion pairs of disposable chopsticks are used and thrown away every year. In China and Japan, manufacturers harvest over 20 million trees to keep up with that demand. So, while chopsticks aren't the biggest threat to the environmet, we should definitely avoid deforestation for the purpose of single-use disposables.
