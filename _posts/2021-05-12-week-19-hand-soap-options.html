We all know how important it is to wash our hands and we should be doing it multiple times a day. The next time you need a hand soap or sanitizer refill, opt for one of these sustainable options.

Blueland Hand Soap Tablets

{% include image-source-set.html image="19-hand-soap/blueland-hand-soap.jpg" alt="blueland brand glass bottle with hand soap refill tablets" %}

Blueland made a splash on the hit TV show, Shark Tank, when they offered consumers a way to avoid plastic waste from cleaning bottles by instead plopping a tablet into a bottle you alread have. You can purchase a glass pump bottle with three soap tablets for $14 or opt for just therefills, which come in compostable packaging. A 9 pack of refills, the most cost effective option, also costs $14

Plaine Products Hand Soap and Sanitizer

{% include image-source-set.html image="19-hand-soap/plainee-products-soap.jpg" alt="plaine products brand hand soap in aluminum bottle" %}

Plaine Products offers both hand soap and sanitizer in infinitely recyclable aluminum bottles with an optional pump. The best part is that you can print out a free shipping label to send the bottles right back to the company, where they will clean and reuse them! You can find a 16 oz bottle of hand soap for $20 or a 16 oz bottle of hand sanitizer for $15 on their website.

Dr. Bronner's Bar Soap

{% include image-source-set.html image="19-hand-soap/dr-bronners-soap.jpg" alt="dr bronner's brand bar soap" %}

There are a lot of bar soap options on the market, but I love Dr. Bronner's because it is all natural, cruelty-free, organic, and biodegradable. The soap bars come in 10 scent options and a 100% post-consumer recycled wrapper. Dr. Bronner's can be found at many chain and grocery stores including Target and REI, or on you can buy directly from their website. The soap bars run about $5 dollars a pop.

Package Free Hand Sanitizer

{% include image-source-set.html image="19-hand-soap/package-free-hand-sanitzer.jpg" alt="glass spray bottle of package free brand hand sanitizer" %}

Package Free Shop offers hand sanitizer in a recyclable glass bottle so we no longer have to depend on the little plastic ones. You can find a 2 oz spray bottle for $12 on their website.

Liquid Castile Soap

{% include image-source-set.html image="19-hand-soap/castile-soap.jpg" alt="gallon jug of vermont country soap brand liquid castile soap" %}

Castile soap is non-toxic, organic, vegetable based jack of all trades. It can be used to clean your body, your house, and even your pets! Castile soap comes unscented or in a variety of all natural scents and you can purchase a one gallon jug from Package Free Shop for $60. Yes, this bulk castile soap comes in a recyclable plastic bottle, but it is still a natural, low-waste alternative to many little hand-soap bottles.
