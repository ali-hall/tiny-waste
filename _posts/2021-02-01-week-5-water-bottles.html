Boy oh boy, plastic water bottles. Where do I begin?

First of all, the US alone consumes 1,500 plastic water bottles every second and 38 billion end up in a landfill every year. Globally, humans buy 1,000,000 plastic bottles per minute.

Now, let's talk economics. Bottled water costs 1,000 times more than tap water. In fact, 90% of the cost is for the bottle. In other words, when we purchase bottled water, we are basically just paying for a plastic bottle.

But the reason we're all here is for the environmental impact and trust me when I tell you, it's not pretty.
