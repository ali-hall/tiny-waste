Tooth Tablets

When it comes to zero waste toothpaste, tablets are hands down my favorite. There is no gel or liquid, which means they are travel friendly and don't make the same mess as traditional toothpaste. I also appreciate that you use one tablet per brush, so you know exactly how many brushes you can get. All you do is pop the tablet it in your mouth and chew until foamy, then you're ready to brush!

  3. 3. Unpaste Tooth Tablets

    Unpaste Tooth Tablets are ideal for anyone looking to keep fluoride in their dental care routine. These tablets come in a backyard compostable bag with 125 tablets for $12.75.

  4. 4. WELdental Chewtabs

    WELdental Chewtabs come in four flavor choices: Peppermint, Cinnamon, Vanilla Mint, and Bubble Gum. You can order them in reusable/recyclable glass bottles or opt for the refills which come in cardboard boxes and commercially compostable bags. A jar with 60 tablets costs $10.95. Chewtabs are made with all natural ingredients and do not contain flouride.
